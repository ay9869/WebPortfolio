About Me

A tale of turning coffee-fueled coding marathons into data-driven wizardry.

Hello! I'm ABHIMANYU YADAV, a graduate student pursuing my Master of Science in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management at the University of Texas at Dallas. With a passion for data analytics and business intelligence, I specialize in uncovering actionable insights from data to drive impactful decision-making.

I bring professional experience from ZS Associates, where I contributed to pharmaceutical consulting by leveraging analytics tools like Advanced Excel, Python, and SQL. My work included analyzing physician and account-level datasets of the US market, optimizing revenue strategies, and executing impactful data-driven projects, such as an injectable HIV drug plan for a Fortune 500 pharmaceutical company. I also have experience in creating interactive dashboards, managing databases, and utilizing tools like Power BI, Tableau, and Python to uncover actionable insights and enhance system performance.

Beyond work, I’m a tech enthusiast who loves diving into the latest gadgets and innovations. When I’m not geeking out over new technologies, you’ll find me wandering through new places. I’m also an avid soccer player—although my skills might occasionally make people question if I’m playing soccer or dodgeball. And when it’s time to unwind, I channel my inner artist, creating vector illustrations in Adobe Illustrator.

If you're looking for someone with a knack for blending technical expertise with strategic thinking, feel free to connect with me or explore my portfolio and projects. I'm always excited to collaborate on challenging opportunities!
